Case Study




Find Me Gluten Free

Find Me Gluten Free is an app based organization that provides an easy solution for people having a hard time eating out while following a gluten-free diet. FMGF is an easily accesible solution through apps on IOS and Andriod.

Jason Elmore founded the company in 2010 aiming to help people eat gluten free, wherever they are. Jason saw a problem with traveling or socializing while on a gluten-free diet so he created the company as a means to make those people feel just as comfortable. The foundation aims to raise awareness for celiac diseas and other food sensitivites.


Most Effecting Method

Previous Human accounts and feedback act as a resource for those looking for GF alternatives and options.

As restuarants and stores remain open through customres buying products, those customers can proved their opinions and knowlege of the products. Simmilarly, gluten-free poeple can share their acounts with other gluten-free people about restuarants and stores.



Ultimately, the most effective thing is to provide people with access to resources that can help them find gluten-free options and alternatives in their area.


Individual store information on dietary accomodations can provide GF people with food options.

When a gluten free person wants to eat out with friends or alone, they must first research the restaurant for options to eat before hand incase the restaurant doesn't acommidate a GF diet. Having restaurant accommidations easily accessible provides options that a gluten-free person can dine or shop at comfortably.




Use of Technology

Q and A sites provide safe places where gluten-free people can ask other poeple about gluten free alternatives, recipes, and eating places.

subreddits and apps act as a community where people can ask questions about certain dietary restriction and alternatives. With real feedback from real people, accurate acounts of restaurants can be provided about restaurant dietary restriction options.



Technology can be used to provide information, provide alternatives, and spread awareness about gluten free foods and recipes.


Find Me Gluten Free provides a real map with restuarant locations and dietary restriction information.

Having this information easily attainable really helps gluten-free people eat out in new places, with friends, and on vacation. The map provides both location and user reports on restuarant accommidations.




Implications

Having a general knowlege of gluten-free diets can help people shop and dine while following a gluten-free lifestyle.

On my project I will have basic knowlege about the gluten-free diet so those shopping can know exactly what falls within their diets. Having a general understanding of gluten-free foods can also improve the food quality made by new gluten-free people.

Recipes are direct checklists that can follow dietary restrictions while creating good tasting food.

On my project I will have a recipe sharing feature where recipes can be shared and viewed at ease. Providing recipes can improve the cooking of gluten-free people and introduce new gluten-free people to alternatives.
